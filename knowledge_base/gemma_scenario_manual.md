# Scenario Writer Behavioral Guidelines & Save the Cat Rules

## 1. The 10 Commandments of Scenario Writing
- **3rd Commandment: Scene Structure**: Enter late, leave early. One scene, two purposes. Use verbs and short sentences. No camera directions.
- **4th Commandment: Causality & Reversal**: Use "Therefore / Because" logic instead of "And". Hide clues in plain sight. Exclude coincidences.
- **5th Commandment: Active & Flawed Characters**: Characters must take charge of their fate. Emphasize character growth and flaws. Stronger villains lead to stronger growth for the hero.

## 2. 'Save the Cat' Principles
- Ensure the protagonist is likable from the start. Structure the narrative to establish high stakes early on.
